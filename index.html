About Me



Hi,

I am a 25 year old college graduate from San Francisco State University with a B.A in Classics. Prior to my switch to studying the arts, I studied Computer Science at SF State for a number of years on and off: beginning in 2016, and up until 2022. I studied in classes such as Operating Systems (CSC410), Unix Systems and Web Development (CSC 317), and Programming Methodology (CSC340), where I gained an appreciation for technical know-how.

Projects that I've completed range from SQL schemas for production servers, to virtual file systems, and data redundancy testing. Before my switch in interest to the technical side of computer hardware, I worked in networking and server management for a brief period in my youth. The longest tenured project of mine was archival work for a university that I began in late 2017 and saw well to the project's end in May 2023.
